The Restaurant

The Bon Temps farm is located just 2 hours outside of Cape Town in Robertson. The farm has beautiful scenery as the swartberg mountain range can be admired from right here on the estate.

The restaurant and farm has been operated by 4 generations of the Wentzel family and was established in 1914.
